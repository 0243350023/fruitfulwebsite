About Us

Welcome to Fruitful Family International Ministry

The Fruitful Family International Ministry was born when the lord spoke to Presiding Bishop and Rev. Mrs. Agyei Frimpong to raise for him a generation of Believers who prize God's word above all things. The Lord reitirated to them the problem of the Church: Ignorance, as stated in HOSEA 4:6. The Lord said to them, "I made life to be enjoyed, not to be endured and yet many of my children are only struggling through life."

The Presiding Bishop and his Wife discovered from the Scriptures that from the beginning God blessed man to be fruitful and productive in life (Genesis 1:28). Jesus, our Lord and Saviour, also came that we might have and enjoy life (John 10:10). However it takes Revelation knowledge of our Lord Jesus Christ for any man to have and enjoy this fruitful life. Presiding Bishop and Mama understood that every man needs to kmow why Jesus came, what His suffering, death and resurrection have accomplished for all mankind. This, then became passion, to bring the knowledge of the benefits of Christ's vicarious sacrifice to all. This is the reason for establishing the Fruitful Family International Ministry.

Our Mission

Bringing appropriate and relevent knowledge of the benefits of Christ's Vicarious sacrifice to all, for a Fruitful and Productive life.

Our Vision

Building a triumphant Church of enlightened believers who live Fruitful and Productive lives, displaying the glory of God everywhere through understanding of their identity in Christ.
